My Journey

My journey began in middle school when I started playing video games on PC's that required more help from other extensions. I found it so interesting that even though game developers could create a game, other people out there would create their own version of the game/extension to alter the same game. Ever since, I have wanted to learn software development and practice more but I never had the proper resources to dive in completely. I went through college with this "pipe dream" on the back burner thinking it was almost impossible to attain. I've had multiple jobs in healthcare and retail since the college days but now I'm taking this opportunity through my current employer to put all my attention and learn what I have always been wanting to. I feel like I've barely scratched the surface with all that is software development so I'm trying to gain all the knowledge I can and when I'm not, I will try practicing everything I've learned.
